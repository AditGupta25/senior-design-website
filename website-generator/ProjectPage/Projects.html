The CCI senior project is a competition for outstanding CCI senior project development groups, which showcases the integration and application of their undergraduate degree program into the senior project. They meet in a competition of the four best projects throughout the College of Computing and Informatics.

Caregiver

Caregiver is an application with the primary goal of reminding children with learning disabilities to perform certain tasks based on strategically placed bluetooth beacons in order to become more independent.
